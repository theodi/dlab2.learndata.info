Welcome

Welcome to the Tanzania Data Lab (dLab) eLearning programme, implemented in collaboration with the University of Dar es Salaam and the Open Data Institute. This programme of courses has been designed to enable you get the most from your data. This programme is free and should take a maximum of 2-3 hours to complete.

Scroll down to discover each of the available lessons

Foundations in data management

Incorrect use of spreadsheets is a key challenge for data quality. Data that is not well managed cannot answer important questions or be used easily. Good management of data makes it easy to ensure data quality, and analyse and visualise results. This course will enable you to organise spreadsheet data, use tools to filter & analyse data and produce an interactive data analysis.

Data visulisation

Chosing the correct visulisation for data can be a challenge, this course is designed to help you explore how to visulise and map data in Tanzania. Each module contains a number of exercises and should take between 1-2 hours to complete including exercises.
